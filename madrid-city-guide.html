06 / Where to Eat

Tapas, Tortillas & Terrace Beers

Madrid eats late. Lunch from 14:00, dinner from 21:00. Order cañas (small beers) and raciones (sharing plates) at the bar. Don't sit down unless you're eating a full meal.

Madrid Essentials

sports_bar Caña

Small draft beer. ~€2–3. The default order at any bar. Just say "una caña, por favor".

wine_bar Tinto de Verano

Red wine + lemon Fanta. Sounds wrong, tastes perfect. Order this instead of sangria; locals don't drink sangria.

local_cafe Café con Leche

The standard coffee order. For good beans, seek out Acid Bakehouse or Pum Pum near the hotel.
